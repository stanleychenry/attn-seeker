# Business Context — attn:seeker

## The Business
- **Name:** attn:seeker (stylised lowercase)
- **Founded:** October 2019
- **Location:** Auckland, New Zealand
- **Type:** Organic social media agency + education platform

## What We Do
attn:seeker is an organic social media agency. We manage the entire social media operation for our clients — strategy, creative, production, social media management, and community management. We don't do paid ads. Everything we do is organic.

We also run an education platform with three products:
- **Live webinars** — entry-level paid events teaching the ERC system
- **ERC Blueprint** — a 1-hour call where we help someone identify their Easily Repeatable Content series
- **The Cohort Programme** — a monthly coaching programme for business owners (see strategy.md)

## Scale and Proof Points
- 1B+ organic views generated across client accounts every year
- 1.4M followers across attn:seeker's own social media platforms
- 30,000 daily newsletter subscribers (marketing newsletter)
- Clients across New Zealand, Australia, the US, UK, and UAE

## The ERC System
The core methodology behind everything attn:seeker does. One repeatable content format with a single changing variable, posted consistently. Creates massive scale without constant creative reinvention. This is the IP that underpins both the agency and the education products.

## Revenue Streams
1. **Agency clients** — full-service social media management (strategy through to community management)
2. **Education platform** — webinars, ERC Blueprint, cohort programme
